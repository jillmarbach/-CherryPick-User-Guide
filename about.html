About Cherry Picking

Cherry Picking is short, multimedia story about Mayes' experience with a dating app called CherryPick. The story details her love-hate relationship with online dating and the challenges of trying to find a relationship right now when so many people are so available, yet it feels impossible to actually connect with anyone. This story is formatted across several webpages with visuals, gifs, and audio incorporated throughout. I wrote, coded, designed, and edited everything in Cherry Picking, including the app design and branding, the page layouts, and the concept behind CherryPick's bunches.

I want to give a huge thank you to everyone that helped me along the way with this project, especially the boys from my dating app days. This story wouldn't exist without you.

If you have feedback, questions, or if something doesn't look quite right, you can contact me at jillianmarbach@gmail.com or see more of my stuff at jillmarbach.com.
